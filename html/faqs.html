FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about X.

What is X? Who can attend?
X is The Mother’s International School’s annual technology symposium. X is an exclusive invite-only event, and invites are extended to a select number of the very best schools in New Delhi, based on significant achievements in the previous years.
When is X? How do I register?
X will be held on August 16–17, 2017. Registrations begin at 8am and the events start soon after. You can register at https://minet.co/rsvp with you invite code.
My school hasn’t recieved an invite code yet. What do I do?
We’re sending out invite codes on a rolling basis. If you school hasn’t recieved an invite code, you can request one at https://minet.co/invite. Since we don’t have a lot of spots for attendees, we’re going to be very selective in sending out invites.
How does the scoring system work?
Winning each event rewards teams with a certain amount of funding, which contributes to their overall valuation:
  • 1st: $100,000
  • 2nd: $75,000
  • 3rd: $50,000
Teams can also raise funds by the following means:
  • $5,000 for each level completed in CryptX.
  • Upto $50,000 from each investor in the fundraising session on Day 2.
The team with the maximum valuation wins the overall prize.
Can I participate in clashing events?
Yes.
Is there a maximum number of events one person can participate in?
No.
